## Best Practices

### Do's
‚úÖ Send consistently (same day each month)
‚úÖ Keep it scannable with headers/bullets
‚úÖ Include specific asks
‚úÖ Share bad news early
‚úÖ Use charts for complex data
‚úÖ Thank investors who helped
‚úÖ Be authentic and transparent

### Don'ts
‚ùå Exceed 3 pages/5 min read time
‚ùå Bury the lede
‚ùå Use too much jargon
‚ùå Only share when things are good
‚ùå Forget to proofread
‚ùå Send attachments (embed instead)
‚ùå Use investor updates for selling

### Response Management
```markdown
Typical Responses to Expect:
‚Ä¢ 10-20% will reply
‚Ä¢ Most popular: offering help with asks
‚Ä¢ Questions about metrics/challenges

‚Ä¢ Advice on specific issues

```
