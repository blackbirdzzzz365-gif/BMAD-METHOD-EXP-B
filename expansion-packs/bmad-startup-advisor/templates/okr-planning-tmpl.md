## Common OKR Pitfalls

### What to Avoid
```markdown
## âš ï¸ OKR Anti-Patterns

### Bad Objectives
âŒ "Improve engineering" (too vague)
âœ… "Build a world-class engineering culture"

âŒ "Make more money" (uninspiring)
âœ… "Become the revenue leader in our category"

### Bad Key Results
âŒ "Launch new feature" (task, not outcome)
âœ… "Achieve 50% adoption of new feature"

âŒ "Improve customer satisfaction" (not measurable)
âœ… "Increase NPS from 32 to 50"

âŒ "Try harder" (not specific)
âœ… "Reduce response time from 24hr to 2hr"

### Process Mistakes
- Too many OKRs (>3-5 per level)
- Cascading instead of aligning
- Treating as performance review
- Setting and forgetting
- Only top-down planning
- Sandbagging targets
```
